Welcome to the Shipping Forecaster

This project is a series of explorations on the Shipping Forecast *.


You can use the words and sounds of the Shipping Forecast to write your own poem.

Or maybe you just want to listen to the latest forecast read by a choir.


* The Shipping Forecast is a weather forecast for the seas around the British Isles.

It is broadcast by BBC Radio 4 and produced by the Met Office.

You can read more about the Shipping Forecast, or read more about this project.
